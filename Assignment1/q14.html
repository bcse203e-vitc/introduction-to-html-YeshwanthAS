Cooking Techniques

Sautéing
Cooking food quickly in a small amount of oil or butter over high heat.
Boiling
Cooking food in water or broth at 100°C, causing it to bubble rapidly.
Grilling
Cooking food over direct heat, usually on a metal grate or grill surface.
Baking
Cooking food using dry heat in an oven, ideal for breads, pastries, and casseroles.
Steaming
Cooking food using the vapor from boiling water, which preserves nutrients and moisture.
