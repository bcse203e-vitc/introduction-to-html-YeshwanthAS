Steps to Drive a Car

  1. Adjust your seat and mirrors
  2. Fasten your seatbelt
  3. Start the engine
  4. Put the car in gear
  5. Check surroundings and mirrors
  6. Release the brake and accelerate slowly
  7. Follow traffic rules and stay alert
